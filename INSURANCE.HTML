HSBC Insurance is provided by Aviva Insurance Limited and is designed to offer you the protection you need when you move away from home for the first time.


More details

4 levels of cover from £25 per year

You can choose cover limits between £2,000 and £5,000 and protect yourself from the financial cost of loss or damage to your contents while you are a student. Cover includes fire, storm, flood and theft following forcible and violent entry/exit from your accommodation.

Accidental damage protection

We’ll cover your home entertainment equipment, mirrors and glass up to £250 per incident. We’ll also cover home computers (not laptops), games consoles, receiving aerials, dishes and CCTV cameras that are fixed to the building.

Optional extras

You can also choose these optional extras:


Personal belongings: covers items you wear or carry outside your accommodation (within the British Isles)

Personal computer equipment: covers equipment in your possession in and away from your accommodation, including installed software (excluding mobile phones)

Pedal cycles: covers loss or damage anywhere within the British Isles as long as your cycle is locked to an immovable object or in a locked building that only you access
